ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Carmelo. I studied a Dual Degree in Computer Engineering and Mathematics at the University of Murcia. I specialized in in Software Engineering.

I love science in general, puzzles and brain teasers in particular. That's where my passion for programming comes from. For me programming is like solving a challenge where the important thing is not only to give a solution, but to do it in a beautiful way, with quality.

In my free time, apart from programming, I like soccer, more to watch it than to play it; the sea and the mountain. Every Sunday you can find me in my seat at the Cartagonova stadium, cheering on F.C. Cartagena.
